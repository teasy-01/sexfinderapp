Safety Guidelines

Tips to help you stay safe while dating online

Protect Your Personal Information

Never share sensitive information like your home address, financial details, or workplace until you've established trust. Keep conversations on the platform initially.

Meet in Public Places

Always choose busy, public locations for first meetings. Let a friend or family member know your plans and check in with them during your date.

Trust Your Instincts

If something feels off, it probably is. Don't hesitate to end a conversation or date if you feel uncomfortable. Your safety comes first.

Watch for Red Flags

Be cautious of users who avoid video calls, ask for money, pressure you to move off-platform quickly, or seem too good to be true.

Use Built-in Communication Tools

Keep conversations within the app until you're comfortable. Our platform has safety features that help protect you during early interactions.

Report Suspicious Activity

Help keep our community safe by reporting inappropriate behavior, fake profiles, or suspicious activity. We review all reports promptly.

Be Aware of Scams

Romance scams are a serious issue in online dating. Never send money to someone you haven't met in person, no matter how convincing their story. Be wary of anyone who claims to be in a crisis or asks for financial help. If someone pressures you for money or personal information, report them immediately.
