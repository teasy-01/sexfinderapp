When it comes to online dating, figuring out if someone’s just looking for fun or something serious can feel like decoding a secret message. 1. Their Bio Is Short and Suggestive

If their bio skips deep details and leans toward playful, flirty one-liners like “here for a good time, not a long time,” that’s your first clue. People looking for hookups usually keep things light and to the point.

2. They Don’t Waste Time on Small Talk

Someone serious about relationships might want long conversations first. Hookup-minded users, on the other hand, tend to cut to the chase — quick compliments, bold questions, and direct invites to meet up.

3. Late-Night Messages Are Their Thing

If they’re mostly messaging you after 10 p.m., it’s not just coincidence. Late-night chatting is a classic sign they’re feeling flirty and looking for something casual.

4. They Keep the Conversation Playful

Serious daters dig into life goals. Hookup daters stick to teasing, emojis, and compliments — the kind of fun back-and-forth that keeps the spark alive without getting too personal.

5. They Drop Subtle (or Not-So-Subtle) Hints

Comments like “we’d have fun together” or “you look like trouble” aren’t random — they’re invitations. Pay attention to tone and timing; it often says more than the words themselves.

6. They’re Not Asking About Your Future Plans

If they’re not diving into where you’ll be in five years or what your family’s like, they’re keeping it casual. Hookups are about chemistry, not commitment.

7. They’re Active on Multiple Platforms

People looking for hookups often stay active across a few dating apps. It’s not disinterest — it’s just part of the casual scene.
